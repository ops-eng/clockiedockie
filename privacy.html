Privacy Policy — ClockieDockie

Last updated: April 19, 2026

ClockieDockie is a macOS app that displays a clock in your Dock and offers optional Pro features via a one-time in-app purchase.

Data Collection

ClockieDockie does not collect, store, or sell personal data.

Local Data

Your app preferences (such as appearance settings and reminder configuration) are stored locally on your Mac.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, ClockieDockie requests notification permission from macOS. Reminder data is handled locally on your device.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card details.

Third-Party Services

ClockieDockie does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
