# 🕒 Clockie Dockie for macOS  
### A Beautiful, Minimal, Always‑Visible Clock for Your Dock

Clockie Dockie is a tiny, elegant macOS utility that does one thing — and does it perfectly:  
**it puts a clean, readable, customizable clock right in your Dock.**

No more checking the menu bar.  
No more hunting for the time on external monitors.  
Clockie Dockie keeps the time exactly where your eyes already are — the Dock.

It’s simple. It’s delightful. It’s surprisingly useful.

---

## ✨ Why Clockie Dockie Exists

macOS doesn’t let you put a clock in the Dock.  
Clockie Dockie fixes that — with style.

Whether you’re working full‑screen, using multiple displays, or just prefer a minimalist menu bar, Clockie Dockie gives you a **persistent, glanceable clock** that stays visible no matter what you’re doing.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 🕒 **Always‑Visible Dock Clock**
- Shows the current time directly in the Dock  
- Works in light and dark mode  
- Perfect for full‑screen apps and multi‑monitor setups  

### 🎨 **Customizable Appearance**
- Choose between multiple clock styles  
- Adjust font size and weight  
- Pick your preferred color theme  
- Clean, modern, and unobtrusive design  

### ⚙️ **Smart Settings**
- Toggle seconds on/off  
- Choose 12‑hour or 24‑hour format  
- Optional date display  
- Automatic refresh with minimal CPU usage  

### 💡 **Designed for Focus**
- No notifications  
- No distractions  
- No unnecessary UI  
- Just the time — beautifully presented  

### 🖥️ **Native macOS App**
- Built with Swift + SwiftUI  
- Optimized for Apple Silicon  
- Lightweight and efficient  
- Zero background noise, zero clutter  

### 🔐 **Privacy‑First**
- No analytics  
- No tracking  
- No internet connection required  
- Your time stays on your device
