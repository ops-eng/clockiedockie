Common Questions

How do I open Settings?

Use ⌘, while the app is active, or open Settings from the Dock menu.

Why are Appearance and Reminders locked?

Those features are part of the Pro Lifetime unlock.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → General → Pro, then click Restore Purchases.

Reminders are not appearing

Check macOS notification permissions for ClockieDockie in System Settings.
